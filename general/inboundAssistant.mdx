## Creating a New Inbound Assistant

In the Inbound Assistants section of the platform, users can easily set up new inbound assistants to handle incoming calls and messages. This feature allows you to customize and streamline your customer support operations, ensuring efficient and personalized interactions.

## To integrate the inbound assistant link in the Twilio account :

1. Log in to your Twilio account.
2. Go to the <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Phone Numbers</span> section and click on your active <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Twilio number</span>.
3. In the <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Voice Configuration</span> settings, paste the URL link provided for the inbound assistant.
4. Click <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Save Configuration</span> at the bottom to update your Twilio setup.

After refreshing the page, you will see the updated Twilio configuration reflecting the integration of the inbound assistant.
